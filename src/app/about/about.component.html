About instire.io

instire.io is an easy and convenient way to get smart insights on the performance of your Instagram profile.

Unlike many other account performance analysis tools available on the Interwebs, instire.io does not confuse you by displaying tons of different values, ratios and charts, but compiles one distinct, descriptive score that can be shared and compared with your friends.

This web application has been created by Paul Haunschmied ( www.paulhaunschmied.com ) as part of the Web Design and Web Programming class during winter semester 2017/2018 at University Of Applied Sciences Upper Austria, Campus Hagenberg.

Please note that instire.io is not affiliated with Instagram, LLC or Facebook, Inc in any way and uses the official Instagram API to retrieve account data from Instagram.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do with instire.io?

instire.io is an easy and convenient way to get smart insights on the performance of your Instagram profile. Find out more about instire.io in our About section.

Why do I need to log in using my Instagram account?

In order to tell instire.io which profile to process, you need to log in via Instagram. By doing so, you furthermore grant instire.io permission to read your profile data and compile metrics on top of that data. Please note that instire.io is not and will never be able to perform actions on your behalf using your Instagram account, which means that you only grant read-only access to your profile and therefore instire.io cannot like, comment, post, delete, etc. any content using your profile. Find out more about the data we store about you in our Privacy Policy.

Can instire.io read my account password?

No. Upon login, you are redirected to the Instagram login page where you enter your Instagram user name and password. That login page is operated by Instagram, therefore you enter your credentials directly at the Instagram website without instire.io being able to read your passwords. After successfully logging in, you are redirected back to instire.io with a token issued by Instagram that grants us read-only access to your profile.

Do you sell my data?

No. instire.io is completely free, we do not display advertisements on our web pages and - of course - we do not sell any of your data to third parties. All your Instagram content belongs to you and we only save your overall score and your username for statistical purposes, but will never publicly display your username/score.

Privacy Policy

Instagram Permissions

instire.io uses the official Instagram API to retrieve profile data from Instagram. In order to tell instire.io which profile to crawl and to grant us permissions to read data of that profile, you need to log in with your Instagram account.

instire.io does not handle any of the login procedures itself. You are simply redirected to the official Instagram login page where you enter your credentials, and since that login page is directly operated by Instagram, there's no way for instire.io to read any of your passwords. After a successful login, Instagram sends back a token to instire.io, which, in turn, allows us to query the Instagram API (more specific, your profile) on your behalf.

After logging in, instire.io is
able to not able to
  • read your Instagram profile picture and account ID.
  • read your Instagram user name, your full name, your bio and your website.
  • read the number of posted media, the number of followers and the number of profiles you follow.
  • read the 20 most recent media (images and videos) posted by you, including the number of likes and comments on a post, the time created, the filters you used, the hashtags you provided, the location associated to the image or video, and the users you tagged in the image or video.
  • like, comment or tag any post on Instagram on your behalf.
  • follow or unfollow accounts on your behalf.
  • send or read direct messages in your name.
  • post any content on your behalf.
  • view, like, comment or create an Instagram Story on your behalf.
  • change any of your profile settings.
  • retrieve data and calculate a score of any profile other than yours.

Data stored by instire.io

instire.io saves your Instagram user name, your Instagram account ID and your score for statistical purposes every time you log in. Your Instagram account ID is stored using encryption, so nobody will be able to read it in the unlikely event of your systems being corrupted by a third party.

Your user name and score will never be displayed publicly and helps other users to get a feeling on how well their profile is performing, relative to previous instire.io users.
